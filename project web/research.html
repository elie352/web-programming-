Research Topics

Explore the following research topics and write your own paper to contribute to the growing knowledge base in web technologies and internet usage, with a special focus on the Middle East and Lebanon.

Internet Infrastructure

Explore the physical and logical components that make up the global internet infrastructure, examining how data travels across networks and the technologies that enable connectivity worldwide.

Key Research Areas:

  • Undersea cable networks and their critical role in global connectivity
  • Internet exchange points (IXPs) and their impact on regional internet performance
  • Content delivery networks (CDNs) and edge computing infrastructure
  • The evolution of network protocols (IPv4 to IPv6 transition)
  • Infrastructure resilience and redundancy measures
  • Environmental impact of internet infrastructure and sustainable solutions

Helpful Resources:

Submarine Cable Map IXP Explainer ITU Statistics PeeringDB

History of the Web

Trace the evolution of the World Wide Web from its origins to the present day, examining key technological breakthroughs, pivotal moments, and the people who shaped its development.

Key Research Areas:

  • The creation of HTML, HTTP, and URLs by Tim Berners-Lee
  • The browser wars of the 1990s and their impact on web standards
  • Web 1.0 to Web 3.0: changing paradigms in user interaction
  • The rise of social media platforms and their transformation of the web
  • Mobile web development and responsive design evolution
  • Regional variations in web adoption and development (with focus on Middle East)

Helpful Resources:

CERN: Birth of the Web W3C History Wayback Machine First Web Browser Simulator

Internet Statistics – Middle East and Lebanon

Analyze internet usage patterns, connectivity statistics, and digital transformation across the Middle East with a special focus on Lebanon, examining growth trends, challenges, and opportunities.

Key Research Areas:

  • Internet penetration rates and growth across Middle Eastern countries
  • Lebanon's internet infrastructure challenges and development
  • Mobile vs. fixed broadband adoption in the region
  • Impact of political and economic factors on internet accessibility
  • E-commerce and digital economy trends in Lebanon and the Middle East
  • Social media usage patterns and platform preferences
  • Digital divide issues within Lebanon and regional disparities

Helpful Resources:

Middle East Internet Stats Lebanon Telecom Authority Digital Reports Statista Data

Research Paper Guidelines

  1. Choose a specific aspect of one of the research topics above that interests you.
  2. Formulate a clear research question that your paper will address.
  3. Conduct thorough research using a combination of academic sources, industry reports, and reliable online resources.
  4. Structure your paper with an introduction, literature review, methodology, findings, discussion, and conclusion.
  5. Ensure proper citations using APA or IEEE format throughout your paper.
  6. Include relevant visuals such as charts, graphs, or diagrams to support your findings.
  7. Aim for a paper length of 2,500-3,500 words (excluding references).
  8. Submit your paper using the form below in PDF format.
